Job Description

Our client, a global leader in the development and production of automotive drive train systems, is seeking a Plant Inventory Analyst. This position will be located in Roxboro, NC.

 

 

Responsibilities- Plant Inventory Analyst

  • Responsible for the maintenance of inventory and cycle count activities.

  • Assist in the daily functions of inventory processes for the facility.

  • Perform cycle count programs and physical inventories.

  • Monitor all store transfers for proper accuracy.

  • Document and report damaged inventory.

  • Maintain and reconcile all data and parameters in QAD.

  • Provide corrective action when required to avoid inventory issues.

  • Support Planners as required.

  • Position reports to the Supply Chain Manager.
